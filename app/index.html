Welcome to 3rdi Resumes

3rdi Resumes aims to be your onw stop shop for all your resume needs. The idea is that you have one place to keep a complete copy of all your job history contacts, recommendations, living history, etc. and you can apply it to customized designer resumes and print them out in pdf format.

The excellent part of all this is the designers we have on board to make your resume stand out, and the complete customization of your resume. For instance you can select a career field such as Administration or Shipping and have all your job histery pertaining to that industry used in the resume rather than everything, so that employers can see what's relevant.
